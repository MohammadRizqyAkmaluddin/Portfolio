Students are welcomed with a clear starting point that sparks curiosity and opens up possibilities to explore. The experience gives them the freedom to discover courses that fit their goals while establishing a sense of trust and direction from the very beginning.

Learners can take their time reviewing details, exploring what’s included, and even trying previews to get a better feel before making a choice. It creates a guided flow where decisions feel more natural and supported, rather than rushed.

The flow continues by simulating how selections turn into actual commitments. Students review their choices, apply available discounts, and walk through a simplified checkout process mirroring a real-world purchase experience without requiring a live payment gateway.

The lecturer account serves as a hub for instructors to create and manage multiple courses, organize content by category and level, and keep track of student enrollment. It also provides insights into sales and earnings, helping lecturers understand the reach of their teaching.

Beyond course management, lecturers can review student project submissions, download files, leave feedback, and assign scores. This combination of teaching tools and evaluation makes it easier to guide students while maintaining control over the courses offered.
